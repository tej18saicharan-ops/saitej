Virat Kohli

Kohli in 2024

Virat Kohli (born 5 November 1988) is an Indian international cricketer and the former captain of the Indian national cricket team. He is a right-handed batter and occasional right-arm medium pace bowler.

Considered one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket, he has been nicknamed the King, the Chase Master, and the Run Machine for his skills, records and ability to lead his team to victory.

Kohli has the most centuries in ODIs and the second-most centuries in international cricket with 85 tons across all formats. He is also the leading run-scorer in the Indian Premier League.

Kohli was the captain of the 2008 U19 World Cup winning team and played a crucial role in India's victories in multiple ICC tournaments.
